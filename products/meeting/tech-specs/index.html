MEETING
Tech Specs

MEETING is a full-sized soundproof room designed for 4-6 people.

With industry-leading acoustics, world-class ventilation, and inspiring design, MEETING is the ideal place to co-create, brainstorm and meet together.

The soundproof meeting room for teams of up to 6

  • Optimal for up to 6 people
  • Soundproof meeting room
  • Speech privacy and perfect acoustics
  • Adjustable ventilation
  • Useful and practical furniture layouts
  • Award-winning design

Optimal meeting space for office and hybrid meetings
MEETING is a modern and compact meeting room. There are two furniture layout options (MEETING Huddle, MEETING Lounge) that have been designed to support all kinds of meeting needs. Or you can choose MEETING without furniture and design your own furniture layout.

Perfect meeting room environment
MEETING Huddle is perfect for information sharing and decision making meetings. It meets all the meeting room requirements.

Ideal for creative and casual meetings
MEETING Lounge is a perfect set-up for creative and coordination meetings. The casual furniture set-up cultivates creativity and relaxed atmosphere.
